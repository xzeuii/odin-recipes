Chopped Cheese on Joe's Pizza THE OCCY WAY!!!

Get that authentic New York Joe's Pizza taste with the same taste as a bodega at the same time!

This recipe is an authentic New York Recipe and has that taste we know and love.

The ingredients required for this burger are:

  • 2 slices of Joe's Pizza
  • 2 Burger Patties
  • Cheese
  • 4 Slices of Bacon
  • Salad
  • Chipotle
  • Mozarella Sticks

Do you want to know how to make a chopped cheese THE OCCY WAY? Let me tell you:

  1. Put 2 slices of Joe's Pizza on top of the grill.
  2. Put 2 patties on top of the grill.
  3. Put the vegetables on top of the grill, next to burgers.
  4. Put the 4 slices of bacon on the grill.
  5. It's time for CHOP CHOP CHOP CHOP. (Chop patties and vegetables together while cooking)
  6. Season chopped ingredients with paprika.
  7. Put the cheese on top.
  8. Put the heaping cheese, vegetable, meat monster on top of the pizza.
  9. Put the bacon on top of the heaping heart attack.
  10. Mozarella sticks too??? You know it!
  11. Salad.
  12. Chipotle.
